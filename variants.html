Review standard

Labels are evidence, not conclusions

“Wave 1-Foil” and “Premium Foil” labels are recorded exactly as issued, but the visible card configuration is evaluated independently.

Image quality

Ambiguity stays unresolved

Glare, compression, low resolution or cropped text can prevent a reliable finding. Unclear examples remain pending rather than being forced into a category.

Publication rule

Variant notes should only become canonical after a comparison entry clearly states the observable difference, documents supporting examples and records any limitations. Submission records may refer to a proposed distinction while remaining pending.
